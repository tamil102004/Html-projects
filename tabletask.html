Chocalate Chip Cookies

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time 10 minutes

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F (180C)
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Chocolate chips 1 cup

Enjoy your homemade cookies!
